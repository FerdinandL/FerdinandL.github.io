I am a second-year graduate student at Stanford focusing on Artificial Intelligence. I have a strong interest in developing novel algorithms in Computer Vision and Speech Processing to solve high impact problems, notably in Healthcare. I am currently part of the Stanford Machine Learning Group, directed by Andrew Ng, and I have been advised in the past by Silvio Savarese, Scott L. Delp and Juliana Freire.

Before joining Stanford I earned a B.S. and a M.S. from Ecole Polytechnique in Computer Science with an Entrepreneurship major. I spent time at LinkedIn and NYU as a Machine Learning Research intern, and at The Boston Consulting Group as a Data Scientist.
